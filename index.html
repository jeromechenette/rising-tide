Welcome to Surf Club

Come Surf With Us!

Humboldt County Family focused surf club

Programs grounded in ocean safety, marine education, social-emotional learning, and cultural connection.

Harnessing the healing power of the ocean to support the physical, emotional, and social well-being of youth and families.

Learn More

Inclusive, family-centered surf clubs that connect youth with the ocean, outdoor learning, and one another.

01.

Surf Skills

Local, family-centered surf clubs that meet regularly to learn, surf, and grow together.

Progressive surf coaching for all experience levels

Emphasis on ocean awareness, safety, and self-regulation

In-water mentorship that builds confidence, strength, and focus.

Surfing becomes both a skill and a practice in mindfulness—teaching balance, resilience, and the joy of being fully present in the sea.

02.

Ocean & Coastal Ed

We integrate marine science, climate science, environmental stewardship, and cultural learning into every program.

Surfers explore the ocean as both classroom and community, deepening their relationship with coastal ecosystems and traditions.

Youth explore the science, stories, and stewardship of the sea through hands-on, place-based learning.


03.

Community Building

We believe lasting impact happens when families and communities learn and grow together.

Our club invites parents and caregivers to be active partners—whether helping in the water, supporting logistics, or exploring shared learning at home. After-school surf programs are 100% volunteer led.

Through regular community events and hands-on service projects, beach clean ups, we foster stewardship, strengthen relationships, and inspire collective care for our coastline and each other.

Afterschool Surf Club

We run a regular parent supported afterschool youth surf club.

We encourage a Sunday Surf community build events.

On-location direct support, with in water supervised surf experimentation.

Target age group 5-13 years old.

Bi-Weekly Sessions; 3:30pm - 6pm at Moonstone Beach

Cohorts launch Mid March & Mid August and run for ~12 weeks

Surf School at Home

Reversed classroom study with a 12 weeks of surf, ocean safety and environmental stewardship, cultural learning, and self-regulation curiculum.

Concepts are introduced at home for discussion and exploration and then reviewed on the beach before putting those skills to use.

Content should be completed each week.

Swim Test Requirement

Swim 100 yards with following times:

  • 7-9 yrs: Complete 100 yards
  • 9-11 yrs: under 2:35 minutes
  • 12-13 yrs: under 2:15 minutes

Tread water for 3 minutes.

Swim 10 yards underwater.

Group Organization

1 Coach per Pod

6 Surfers per Pod

1 Land Team Coach

1 outside break water Coach

Maintaining a 1:4 Coach:Surfer Ratio

Land team 1:8
