North American Coastal Access Alliance

About Us

Empowering and supporting the creation of local family surf clubs to ensure that ocean safety, marine and coastal cultural education, social and emotional learning, surf community building, and surf education are accessible to all. Developing, maintaining, and providing a blueprint to run a regular coach supported bi-Weekly afterschool youth surf club, promoting a community surf build events, providing comprehensive and complimentary at home instructional content, on-location direct instruction, with in-water supervised surf experimentation.

Mission

Supporting the physical, emotional, social, and educational well-being of youth and families. Empowering communities to launch surf clubs rooted in equity, ocean education, social-emotional learning, and family engagement. Through inclusive surf and outdoor programs, combined with ocean safety, marine science education, social-emotional learning, opportunities to travel and experience new environments, community service projects, and family participation, we foster resilience, confidence, stewardship, and a community grounded in joy, curiosity, equity, and respect for the natural world.

Vision

We envision a future where every coastal community offers young people and families—regardless of background or resources—a deep sense of belonging in the ocean and outdoors. Through community-based surf clubs and educators, participants gain the knowledge to navigate and care for marine environments, develop tools for self-awareness and emotional resilience, and experience the joy of surfing. These experiences ripple outward, building confidence, resilience, and broadened perspectives that extend into all areas of life. Parents and families are essential partners in this journey, contributing time and care through discussion of materials and concepts, as well as shared ocean experiences that strengthen community, family bonds, and connection to the natural world.

Family Surf Clubs

Family Surf Clubs are family focused coach-supported youth programs designed to introduce children ages 5–13 to the joy and challenge of surfing. Through supervised in-water sessions, participants develop balance, ocean awareness, and self-confidence while learning to read waves and stay safe in the water. Our program emphasizes skill development, teamwork, and fostering a lifelong connection to the ocean, with opportunities for families to come together for shared surf experiences and community events.

The at-home Surf School program complements in-person sessions with a series of short, engaging activities that families can do together. These explore:

  • Ocean safety and surf science
  • Environmental stewardship and coastal ecology
  • Cultural connections to the sea
  • Emotional awareness and self-regulation

This blended approach encourages families to learn, discuss, and explore together, building skills and confidence that extend from the home to the water. By combining hands-on surf practice with meaningful at-home activities, the program nurtures capable, caring, and ocean-minded young surfers.

Rising Tide Surf Clubs

The Rising Tide Surf Clubs are designed for youth ages 10–15 who are ready to take their surfing to the next level. Building on foundational skills, participants gain greater independence in the water while refining technique, developing strategy, and learning to navigate waves with confidence. The program emphasizes advanced coaching, personal growth, and healthy competition, helping surfers set goals, track progress, and challenge themselves in a supportive environment.

Through structured sessions, young surfers explore:

  • Taking on a leadership role by working with and supporting the Local Family Surf Clubs and their Younger Learnersf
  • Advanced surf techniques and wave reading
  • Ocean safety, awareness, and environmental responsibility
  • Teamwork, leadership, and goal-setting
  • Participation in friendly competitions and skill challenges
  • Builiding on the foundation of Strong SEL and Marine Science understanding

The Rising Tide Surf Clubs blend hands-on coaching with opportunities for self-directed learning and practice, giving participants the freedom to take ownership of their development. Our mission is to nurture skilled, confident, and ocean-minded young surfers who are prepared to ride bigger waves—both on the water and in life.
